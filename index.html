What is RevolUX?

RevolUX is an Inter University UI/UX Designathon organized by AIESEC in University of Colombo together with IEEE Student Branch of University of Colombo School of Computing.

We are proud to bring this event for the second time in a row with the goal of providing undergraduates with hands on experience in User Interface and User Experience designing.

This event would open doors to get exposed to design-related fields that helps to enhance the skills of undergraduates and make themselves ready to step to the world of work with confidence.

Guidelines

  1. All the participants must be undergraduates studying at any university.
  2. One team should comprise of a minimum of 3 members and a maximum of up to 4 members.
  3. All members of the team should be from the same university.
  4. The Initial round selections would be based on the presentation of a solid UI solution by mock-ups and prototypes.
  5. The Final round will be happening physically as a 24-hour Designathon where the final 10 teams will be competing.
